Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Who We Are

Exeter 501 Darts Club is a volunteer-led junior darts club based in Exeter, Devon. We are not a registered business. We operate on a not-for-profit basis, run entirely by parent volunteers for the benefit of young players aged 6–18.

If you have any questions about this policy or how we handle your data, you can contact us at hello@exeter501dartsclub.co.uk.

What Information We Collect

When you sign up to the club or register your child for sessions, we may collect:

  • Parent/guardian name and contact details (email address, phone number)
  • Child's name and age
  • Emergency contact information
  • Any relevant medical or additional needs information you choose to share with us

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely for the purposes of:

  • Managing club membership and attendance
  • Contacting you about sessions, changes to schedule, or club news
  • Ensuring the safety and wellbeing of your child during sessions
  • Contacting emergency contacts if needed

We will never use your information for marketing purposes unrelated to the club, and we will never sell or share your data with third parties.

How We Store Your Information

Your information is stored securely and only accessible to the club's organising volunteers who need it to run sessions safely. We use reasonable measures to protect your data, but as a small volunteer-led club we cannot guarantee the same level of security as a large organisation. We retain your information only for as long as your child is an active member, and will delete it upon request or when it is no longer needed.

Your Rights

Under UK data protection law, you have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you or your child
  • Ask us to correct any inaccurate information
  • Ask us to delete your data
  • Withdraw your consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please email us at hello@exeter501dartsclub.co.uk.

Third-Party Services

We use Google Forms to collect sign-up information. When you submit a form, your data is processed by Google in accordance with their own privacy policy. We do not use cookies or any tracking technologies on this website.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
