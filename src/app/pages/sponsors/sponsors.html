Our Sponsors

Exeter 501 Darts Club exists because people in our community believe in giving young players a chance. We are entirely volunteer-run, and every bit of support we receive goes directly into making sessions better, safer, and more accessible for the kids.

None of this would have been possible without the generosity of those who have stepped up to back us. To every sponsor who has put their trust in what we are building: thank you. You are helping shape the next generation of darts players in Devon.

Devon County Sports

Wes and his team at Devon County Sports have backed our club from day one. Their genuine enthusiasm for grassroots darts and their willingness to support what we are building has been instrumental in getting Exeter 501 Darts Club off the ground.

Based in Paignton, Devon County Sports is widely regarded as the best darts shop in Devon. They stock a huge range from all the top names: Target, Winmau, Harrows, Unicorn, Red Dragon, L-Style, and more. They even have a full darts setup in-store so you can try before you buy. It is the kind of personal, knowledgeable service you simply cannot get from an online retailer.

For our young players, having a sponsor like Devon County Sports means access to quality equipment, expert advice, and the confidence that comes from knowing a trusted name in the sport is behind them.
