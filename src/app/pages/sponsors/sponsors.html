Our Sponsors

Exeter 501 Darts Club exists because people in our community believe in giving young players a chance. We are entirely volunteer-run, and every bit of support we receive goes directly into making sessions better, safer, and more accessible for the kids.

None of this would have been possible without the generosity of those who have stepped up to back us. To every sponsor who has put their trust in what we are building: thank you. You are helping shape the next generation of darts players in Devon.

Devon County Sports

When we first started putting Exeter 501 together, we knew we needed a partner who understood darts at a grassroots level. Wes and his team at Devon County Sports did not hesitate. From day one, they have backed our academy with genuine enthusiasm, and their support has been instrumental in getting us off the ground.

Based in Paignton, Devon County Sports is widely regarded as the best darts shop in Devon, and for good reason. They stock an outstanding range from all the top names in the sport: Target, Winmau, Harrows, Unicorn, Red Dragon, L-Style, and more. Whether you are looking for your first set of darts or upgrading to something more refined, their selection of steel tip darts, flights, shafts, dartboards, surrounds, and accessories is second to none.

What sets them apart is the experience of actually visiting the shop. Wes and his team genuinely know their stuff, and they are always happy to talk you through the options. They even have a full darts setup in-store so you can try before you buy. It is the kind of personal, knowledgeable service that you simply cannot get from a faceless online retailer.

For our young players, having a sponsor like Devon County Sports means the world. It means access to quality equipment, expert advice when they need it, and the confidence that comes from knowing a trusted name in the sport is behind them.
