About Us

Exeter's home for junior darts. A parent-run club where young players aged 6–18 can belong, grow, and achieve.

Our Story

Exeter 501 Darts Club was born from a simple observation: there was nothing in Exeter offering what young darts players actually need. No affordable, community-driven club that puts the kids first. No place where beginners and experienced players alike could walk through the door and feel welcome from day one.

So we built it ourselves. A group of parents who believe every child deserves access to quality mentorship, a safe environment, and a genuine chance to fall in love with the sport, without it costing the earth.

We don't run the club for profit. We're parents, volunteers, and darts enthusiasts who saw a gap and decided to fill it. Everything we do is guided by one question: is this genuinely good for the kids?

Our Values

These aren't just words on a wall. They shape every decision we make, every session we run, and every conversation we have.

Community First

We're a club built by parents, for kids. No egos, no politics, just a welcoming group that wants to see every young player thrive.

A Safe Place

Every child's wellbeing comes first. We maintain a supportive, inclusive environment where young players feel safe to try, fail, and grow.

Affordable for All

At just £2.50 a session, we don't want cost to be a barrier. Great darts shouldn't depend on your family's budget.

Total Transparency

Where the money goes, how we make decisions, what we're planning next; you'll always know. No hidden agendas, ever.

Genuine Development

We're not here to babysit. We provide real mentorship and structured practice that helps every young player improve at their own pace.

Fun Comes First

Darts should be enjoyable. If the kids aren't having fun, we're doing something wrong. Improvement is a natural byproduct of loving the game.

What to Expect

1

Turn Up

No booking needed. Just come along to a session with your child. We'll welcome you, show you around, and get them set up.

2

Meet the Mentors

We'll introduce your child to a mentor suited to their ability level, whether they're a complete beginner or already throwing 180s.

3

Play & Learn

Each session is a mix of structured practice, friendly games, and mentorship. It's relaxed, social, and focused on helping every player enjoy their darts.
